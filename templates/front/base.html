CETAA

Formed in 1976 with the aim of establishing rapport with former students and the college and to have common programs which will be mutually beneficial.

CETAA now has about 15,000 members and is increasing every year. Now, CETAA has chapters at Chicago, Dubai, Delhi, Kuwait, Bangalore, America and Kochi.Instituting awards and scholarships to reward excellence in curricular and extra curricular activities has been one of the major activities of CETAA. CETAA day celebration is carried out every year during November.
